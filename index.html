About Me

I'm a passionate junior frontend developer with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, complemented by backend curiosity through Python and C++. I thrive on crafting clean, scalable design systems that elevate user experiences with pixel-perfect precision. A solo builder at heart, I love diving deep into projects using Git and VS Code to bring ideas to life.

As a self-driven perfectionist, I’m always refining my skills and pushing boundaries—whether it’s learning a new framework or experimenting with responsive layouts. My goal is to grow as a freelancer and ultimately join a dynamic startup where I can make meaningful impact and keep evolving with the tech around me.

Blog

Welcome to Code by Pixel—my space to document the imperfect, pixel-perfect journey of becoming a frontend developer. As a junior dev navigating the world of HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, I’ve discovered a passion for crafting interfaces where design and logic meet. Whether it’s building reusable components or refining design systems, I obsess over the details that shape great user experiences. This blog isn’t just about code—it’s about growth, self-learning, and the victories (big and small) along the way.

Here, you’ll find my experiments, lessons, and reflections as I move from solo builds to startup ambitions. I’ll share how I tackle projects, explore new tools, and turn freelance ideas into action. If you’re also learning to code, building in isolation, or dreaming of contributing to something bigger, this blog might feel like home. Let’s build something meaningful—one pixel at a time.
